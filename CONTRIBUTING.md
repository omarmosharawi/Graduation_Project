# Contributing to The RE

Thank you for your interest in contributing to The ReWard project. To ensure the intellectual property of this project remains protected, we have a strict contribution policy.

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Before we can accept any contributions, you must sign a Contributor License Agreement (CLA). This agreement clarifies that any intellectual property you contribute to the project becomes the sole property of the project owner, Omar Mohamed.

This is a necessary step to protect the project and ensure that we have the legal right to use your contribution.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Contact Us**: Before writing any code, please reach out to the project owner to discuss your proposed changes.
2.  **Sign the CLA**: We will provide you with the CLA to sign.
3.  **Fork and Branch**: Once approved, you can fork the repository and create a new branch for your feature.
4.  **Submit a Pull Request**: After completing your work, submit a pull request for review.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in helping us maintain the integrity and legal standing of this project.
