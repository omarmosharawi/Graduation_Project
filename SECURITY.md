# Security Policy for The RE

The security of our platform and the protection of our users' data is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring our systems are secure and compliant with all relevant data protection laws, including Egypt's Data Protection Law No. 151 of 2020 and Saudi Arabia's PDPL.

## Key Security Measures

* **Data Encryption**: All data transmitted between the client applications and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.
* **Secure Authentication**: We use JSON Web Tokens (JWT) to secure our API endpoints and manage user sessions.
* **Infrastructure Security**: Our infrastructure is containerized using Docker and protected by a reverse proxy (Nginx) and firewall rules.
* **Regular Audits**: Our cybersecurity team conducts regular penetration testing and security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please help us by reporting it responsibly. We appreciate your efforts to disclose your findings privately so we can address them before they are exploited.

Please email the details of the vulnerability to our security team at `info@nova-dev-company.com`.

We will make every effort to acknowledge your report, investigate the issue, and take corrective action as quickly as possible.
