# How to Use REward App

This guide explains how to use the REward recycling app to earn points and redeem rewards.

---

## 📱 Getting Started

### 1. Download & Install

- Download the REward app from the Google Play Store (coming soon)
- Or install the APK directly from the development team

### 2. Create an Account

1. Open the app and tap **"Get Started"**
2. Choose **"Sign Up"** to create a new account
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Accept the Terms of Service
5. Tap **"Create Account"**

### 3. Login

If you already have an account:
1. Enter your email/phone
2. Enter your password
3. Tap **"Sign In"**

---

## ♻️ How to Recycle

### Finding a Kiosk

1. Tap the **Map** icon in the bottom navigation
2. View nearby REward kiosks on the map
3. Tap a kiosk marker to see:
   - Opening hours
   - Distance from you
   - Operational status
4. Tap **"Get Directions"** to navigate there

### At the Kiosk

**Option A: Using QR Code**
1. Tap the **QR Code** button (center of bottom nav)
2. Show your QR code to the kiosk scanner
3. Wait for the kiosk to recognize you

**Option B: Using Phone Keypad**
1. Enter your registered phone number on the kiosk keypad
2. The kiosk will verify your account

### Depositing Items

Once identified:
1. Insert plastic bottles into the bottle slot
2. Insert aluminum cans into the can slot
3. Watch your points increase in real-time!
4. When done, press the **"Finish"** button on the kiosk

**Points System:**
- 🧴 Plastic Bottle = **1 point**
- 🥫 Aluminum Can = **2 points**

## 💰 Earning & Tracking Points

### View Your Points

1. Go to **Home** or **Profile** screen
2. See your:
   - **Current Points** - Available to spend on rewards
   - **Total Points** - Lifetime recycling achievements

### Points History

1. Go to **Profile** > **Points History**
2. View all your transactions:
   - Points earned from recycling
   - Points spent on rewards

---

## 🎁 Redeeming Rewards

### Browse Rewards

1. Go to **Home** screen
2. Browse **Featured Offers** carousel
3. Or tap a **Category** to filter offers

### Redeem a Reward

1. Find an offer you want
2. Check the points required
3. Tap the offer to view details
4. Tap **"Redeem"** to confirm
5. Get your unique voucher code
6. Show the code at the partner store!

## 🏆 Leaderboards & Ranks

### Ranks

Your rank is based on **Total Points** (lifetime):

| Rank | Points Required |
|------|-----------------|
| 🥉 Bronze | 0+ |
| 🥈 Silver | 1,000+ |
| 🥇 Gold | 5,000+ |
| 💎 Platinum | 10,000+ |

Higher ranks unlock **better discounts** on rewards!

### Leaderboards

1. Tap **Ranking** in the bottom navigation
2. Switch between **Weekly** and **All-Time**
3. See your position and compete with others!

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### QR Code Not Scanning?

- Increase screen brightness
- Hold phone steady
- Clean the kiosk scanner lens
- Try using phone number instead

### Bluetooth Connection Issues?

- Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
- Stay within 5 meters of the kiosk
- Restart the app and try again

### Points Not Showing?

- Wait a few seconds for sync
- Pull down to refresh the Home screen
- Check your internet connection

### Need More Help?

1. Go to **Profile** > **Help Center**
2. Browse FAQs
3. Contact support if needed
