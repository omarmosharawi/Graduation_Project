The Problem

Egypt recycles less than 20% of its waste

Over 90 million tons of solid waste are generated annually, with most ending up in landfills. The lack of incentive and infrastructure keeps recycling rates dangerously low.

The Solution

The RE makes recycling rewarding

We combine smart kiosk technology with a gamified rewards system. Users earn real points for every recyclable they deposit — redeemable for discounts, mobile credit, or charitable donations.

How It Works

Four simple steps to start earning rewards for recycling.

1

Sign Up

Create your account and get your unique QR code in seconds.

2

Find a Kiosk

Use the interactive map to locate nearby smart recycling kiosks.

3

Drop your recyclables into the kiosk and scan your QR code.

4

Earn & Redeem

Collect points and redeem for discounts, mobile credit, or donations.
