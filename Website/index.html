About ReWard – Recycle + Reward

ReWard is an innovative digital platform designed to promote environmental sustainability by encouraging users to recycle responsibly. Through a seamless mobile app, ReWard connects individuals, communities, and collection partners to make recycling simple, rewarding, and measurable.

Our Mission

To inspire positive environmental behavior by turning waste into value — empowering users to contribute to a cleaner, greener future while earning rewards for their actions.

How It Works (At a Glance)

  • 1. Register: Create an account to start tracking your recycling activities and points.
  • 2. Sort & Identify: Learn which items can be recycled and prepare them for drop-off.
  • 3. Drop or Schedule Pickup: Use the app to locate collection kiosks or request home pickup through our partners.
  • 4. Verify & Earn: Once recyclables are collected, they are verified and points are added to your wallet automatically.
  • 5. Redeem Rewards: Exchange points for shopping vouchers, discounts, or charitable donations.
  • 6. Track Your Impact: View dashboards showing your total CO₂ savings, waste reduction, and contribution to sustainability goals.

Key Objectives

The RE (ReWard) empowers users to make sustainable choices through innovation and transparency. By connecting citizens, local businesses, and recycling facilities, we create an ecosystem where every recycled item has value — both economically and environmentally.

Availability & Company

Territory: ReWard currently operates across Egypt, connecting users with nearby recycling partners, collection points, and eco-centers. The platform is optimized for Egypt's local recycling infrastructure and supports Arabic and English for user convenience.

Developer: ReWard was proudly developed by a dedicated team of Egyptian innovators focused on sustainable technology. The system integrates seamlessly with partner kiosks, community collection programs, and municipal recycling networks nationwide.

Our Purpose

ReWard was created to empower Egyptian citizens to make a measurable impact on their environment by turning recyclable materials into valuable rewards. Our mission is to make recycling simple, rewarding, and accessible to everyone — helping Egypt move closer to a cleaner and greener future.

How We Support Sustainability

ReWard reduces plastic, metal, and electronic waste by incentivizing recycling through direct economic rewards. Each redeemed item supports local recycling chains and contributes to measurable sustainability indicators — tracked in real time within the app for users and partners.

Frequently Asked Questions

What items are accepted?

Plastic bottles (PET), aluminum cans, paper and glass — check the app for full accepted items and prep instructions.

How are points calculated?

Points are awarded per verified item depending on material and campaign bonuses. Special events may grant extra points.

How do I redeem points?

Open the Offers section in the app and choose partner vouchers or donation options. Follow the partner's redemption steps.
